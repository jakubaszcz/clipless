# 🧠 Clipless

**Clipless is a lightweight, persistent clipboard manager designed to remove friction from repetitive workflows.**

Unlike the default Windows clipboard, Clipless allows you to **store, manage, and reuse your copied content over time**, without worrying about losing it.

---

## ✨ Why Clipless?

The Windows clipboard is useful, but limited:
- It only stores a **small history**
- It resets between sessions
- It becomes messy when copying frequently

Clipless solves this by giving you a **controlled, persistent clipboard system**.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 📋 Persistent Clipboard
Store your copied content locally.  
Your clips are saved and available even after restarting your computer.

---

### 🔍 Search Your Clips
No more endless scrolling.  
Quickly find what you need with built-in search.

---

### 📂 Expand Long Clips
Large content is truncated in the UI, but can be expanded anytime for full readability.

---

### 📊 Usage Tracking
Each clip tracks:
- Creation date
- Number of times it has been used

---

### ⚡ Fast Copy Actions
Copy any stored clip instantly with one click.

---

## ⌨️ Controls

Once the application is running:

- **CTRL + ALT + C** → Save selected text into Clipless  
- **CTRL + ALT + D** → Open the Clipless window  

These shortcuts allow you to quickly capture and reuse content without interrupting your workflow.

---

## 🧠 Designed for Productivity

Clipless is especially useful for:
- Sending repetitive emails
- Copying templates
- Managing code snippets
- Any workflow involving repeated copy/paste

---

## 🖥️ Platform

Currently available on:
- **Windows**

Support for other operating systems may come later.

---

## ⚠️ Permissions

Clipless may require **administrator permissions**.

This is because it is designed to behave like a **system-level clipboard tool**.

A non-admin version could be made, but it would not reflect the intended behavior.
